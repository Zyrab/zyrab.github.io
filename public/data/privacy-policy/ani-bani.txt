# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 01.01.2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **ანი ბანი** ("the App") handles information.

## 1. Introduction
**ანი ბანი** is an educational mobile application designed to teach the Georgian alphabet to children approximately aged 3–8, including Georgian-speaking children living abroad.

The App is intended solely for educational purposes.

## 2. Information Collection and Use
The App **does not collect, store, process, or share any personal data**.

Specifically, the App:
- Does not request or collect personal information
- Does not collect names, email addresses, or identifiers
- Does not collect location data
- Does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising services
- Does not transmit data to third parties

## 3. Children’s Privacy
The App is designed for children and respects children's privacy.

Since no personal data is collected, the App does not store, process, or share any information related to children or their guardians.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The App does not integrate or use third-party services that collect or process user data.

## 5. Internet Usage
The App does not require users to provide personal information to function. Any internet access, if present, is not used to collect or transmit personal data.

## 6. Data Security
As the App does not collect or store personal data, no user data is retained or exposed.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated if the App’s functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
