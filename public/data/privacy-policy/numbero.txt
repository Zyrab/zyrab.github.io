# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 01.01.2026

Welcome to **Numbero**. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Numbero mobile game.

## 1. Introduction
Numbero is a game application that uses Google Play Games Services for optional authentication and displays advertisements using Google AdMob.

## 2. Information We Collect
Numbero does not collect personal information such as names or email addresses.

The following data may be processed:

### a) Play Games Services Player ID
If a user chooses to sign in with Google Play Games Services, a unique player identifier (UUID) provided by Google is used to:
- Identify the player
- Store and retrieve game progress
- Enable gameplay-related features such as rankings or progress synchronization

If a user does not sign in, no game-related user data is stored.

### b) Advertising Data
Numbero uses **Google AdMob** to display advertisements.

AdMob may automatically collect:
- Advertising identifiers
- Device information
- Approximate location
- Interaction data related to ads

This data is collected and processed **by Google**, not directly by Numbero.

## 3. How We Use the Information
Collected data is used only to:
- Provide core game functionality
- Store optional game progress
- Display advertisements within the app

No data is used for direct marketing or profiling by the developer.

## 4. Third-Party Services
Numbero uses the following third-party services:

- **Google Play Games Services** – optional user authentication  
- **Firebase Firestore** – storage of game-related data linked to Player ID  
- **Google AdMob** – advertising display  

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

## 5. Information Sharing
Numbero does not sell or share user data with third parties.  
Data is processed only as required to operate the services listed above or when legally required.

## 6. Data Security
All communication between the app and backend services is encrypted. Reasonable measures are used to protect stored data.

## 7. Data Retention and Deletion
Game-related data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the service.

Users may request deletion of their game data by contacting the developer.

## 8. Children’s Privacy
Numbero is designed for children.

- The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children
- Authentication via Play Games Services is optional and handled by Google
- Advertisements are provided by Google AdMob in accordance with Google’s child-directed advertising policies

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and take effect immediately.
